About

I design and evaluate data-driven systems for personalization and decision support, with a focus on recommender systems and contextual modeling.
I am currently completing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Verona advised by Prof. Elisa Quintarelli and Sara Migliorini. During my doctoral studies, I have also spent time as a visiting researcher at Tampere University (Finland), working on fairness and bias in recommender systems, under the supervision of Prof. Kostas Stefanidis.
